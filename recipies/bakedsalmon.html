Salmon

Description

This baked salmon is a great recipe for beginners. This was my first time making fish and it was a hit. Even my 9-year-old daughter who wouldn't ever dream of eating fish had half of my portion!>

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons of light olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) salmon fillets

Directions

  1. Season chicken with a coat of olive oil and add salt and creole seasoning to both sides
  2. Bake chicken in a boiler pan for 30 minutes. Flip after 15 minutes
  3. Once fully cooked serve with chicken broth on the side.
  4. Don't invite your friends over because the will eat all of the food. lol
