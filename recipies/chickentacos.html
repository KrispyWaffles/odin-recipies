Chicken Tacos

Description

Chicken baked in a citrus marinade is placed in a soft flour tortilla and topped with shredded cabbage, cilantro, guacamole, green onion, and salsa. Everyone loves this!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned Salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic Salt
  • 1 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces, or more to taste
  • 1/2 head cabbage, shredded
  • 3 limes, juiced, divided
  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 8 (6 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 bunch green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sour cream, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup sals, or to taste

Directions

  1. Season chicken with a coat of olive oil and add salt and creole seasoning to both sides
  2. Bake chicken in a boiler pan for 30 minutes. Flip after 15 minutes
  3. Once fully cooked serve with chicken broth on the side.
